Leading Construction & Infrastructure Development Company In India

Rithwik Projects Private Limited (RPPL) is an integrated construction, infrastructure development and management company in India. Since the commencement of its business in 1999, the Company has done a range of construction and infrastructure projects in various sectors such as Irrigation, Highways, buildings, Railways, Hydro power and Solar power projects.We continue to aim towards being a leader in the infrastructure industry with strong client relationships, dedicated and talented workforce, with an impeccable reputation in quality and project management.
