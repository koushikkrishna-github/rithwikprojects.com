Corporate Social Responsibility

In collaboration with the government, Rithwik Projects continues to organize workshops for the parents of paralysis patients. Extending financial assistance for the construction and renovation of religious buildings in the Cuddapah District of Andhra Pradesh. 

The construction of 120 houses for poor people in Cuddapah, as well as the construction of approach roads in several villages, Donation of cooking gas connections to the needy in various areas of Rayalaseema and extending financial support for hospital construction in Andhra Pradesh and other states, as well as awarding merit scholarships to deserving students in various parts of Andhra Pradesh. Coordinating school-day activities in Cuddapah Supporting children in Hyderabad and other cities who promote environmentally safe practices.
